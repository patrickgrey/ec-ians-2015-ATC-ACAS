TCAS Operating Principles and Advisories

TCAS II significantly improves flight safety. However, it cannot entirely eliminate all risks of collision. Additionally, it might itself induce a risk of collision. In normal operation, the CAS logic works on a 1-second cycle.

The following description provides a general understanding of these functions.

A complete description of TCAS II version 7.1 logic can be found in the TCAS II MOPS (Minimum Operational Performance Standards) published by RTCA (document DO-185B) in the USA and published by EUROCAE (document ED-143) in Europe.
