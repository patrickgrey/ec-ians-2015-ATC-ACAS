ACAS - An overview

Objectives

The objective of these e-learning modules is to enable air traffic controllers to better manage advisories by:

  • a) understanding how ACAS works;
  • b) anticipating ACAS behavior in their ATM environment;
  • c) understanding the responsibilities of pilots and air traffic controllers during an ACAS event; and
  • d) evaluating the effectiveness and necessity of ACAS events.
